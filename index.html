About Me

WELCOME

Hey there! I'm thrilled to share a bit about myself. I'm a passionate online streamer who enjoys connecting with my audience through gaming, tech talks, and personal vlogs. Whether it's tackling the latest game, sharing coding tutorials, or just hanging out and chatting, I love creating a welcoming and fun environment for everyone who joins my streams. In addition to streaming, I work part-time as a waiter in a catering services. Coding is not just a job for me it's a way to solve problems and bring ideas to life. But there's more to me than just screens and streams. I’m also a dancer! Dancing is my creative outlet and a way to express myself beyond words. Whether it's contemporary, hip-hop, or just freestyling to my favorite tunes, dance brings balance to my life and adds a vibrant beat to my everyday routine. Join me on this multifaceted journey where technology meets creativity, and let's build, play, and dance our way through life's adventures together!.
